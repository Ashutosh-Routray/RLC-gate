Raghunath Learning Center (RLC)
The premier institute for preparing for GATE exam in Electronics and Communication Engineering (EC). Located in Bangalore, RLC provides concept-oriented GATE coaching. The focus is on both the depth of understanding of the concepts, and innovative problem solving techniques. The subjects are taught by PhDs from IISc.









Achievements

Out of the 30 candidates from RLC who wrote GATE-2015, 7 are joining IISc (5 for ME/M.Tech and 2 for research programs). Overall, 28 candidates have qualified, and many are joining IITs and NITs.
